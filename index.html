What We Do


Presentations

We cover a wide range of topics from programming languages to AI and cybersecurity.

Projects

We love working on a variety of projects, from short, fun hands-on demos to real-world applications.

Competitions

We compete in individual and team challenges, from in-house events to ACSL and USACO.

Games

We love to play computer science Jeopardy and Kahoot, among other games.

Discussions

We always have group discussions to share ideas, troubleshoot and collaborate on projects, and explore the latest trends in computer science.

Mentorship

We have several AP CSA and CSP alums as mentors, so feel free to ask for help with anything computer science-related, including classes.
