Why We Started GWC

We started Huron Girls Who Code to create the community we felt was missing. Too often, we found ourselves sitting in classrooms and competitions with hardly any other girls. It could be isolating and discouraging, and we wished for peers who shared our curiosity for computer science. With this club, we hope to build the welcoming space we always wanted—a place where anyone interested in coding can learn, experiment, and grow together, no matter their experience.
